About the Founder

Meet the visionary behind DTECH-NURSE

Nurse Mojisola, popularly known as DTECHNURSE, is the visionary founder of DTECHNURSELTD. She is a passionate nurse with a strong interest in the intersection of healthcare and technology.

Driven by a desire to see nurses grow beyond traditional roles, Mojisola created DTECHNURSE as a platform to educate, inspire, and empower healthcare professionals to embrace digital skills and innovation. She believes that the future of nursing goes beyond the bedside and that technology is a powerful tool that can transform patient care and create new opportunities for nurses.

With a growing passion for health informatics, data analysis, and digital health solutions, she is committed to helping nurses understand and navigate the evolving healthcare landscape. Through her content, she simplifies complex medical and technological concepts, making them easy to understand and apply in everyday practice.

Her mission is clear: to bridge the gap between nursing and technology, and to raise a new generation of nurses who are confident, skilled, and ready for the future of healthcare.

Through DTECHNURSE, she continues to build a community where learning, growth, and innovation are encouraged, helping nurses unlock their full potential both clinically and digitally.

Our Story

From Nursing to Technology

DTECH-NURSE was born from a simple observation: many nurses feel left behind in the digital transformation of healthcare. As a nurse who experienced this challenge firsthand, I decided to create a resource that bridges this gap.

What started as a personal blog has grown into a community of thousands of healthcare professionals who want to embrace technology confidently. We're here to prove that you don't need to be a tech expert to use technology effectively in your nursing practice.

— Nurse Mojisola, Founder
