Who I Am

I'm a nurse who is passionate about technology and how it can transform healthcare. D-Tech Nurse was created to help other healthcare professionals embrace technology in their daily practice.

What I Do

I provide resources, tutorials, and insights about:

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR)
  • Healthcare apps and tools
  • Digital patient engagement
  • Telehealth and remote care
  • Data privacy and security

My Mission

To bridge the gap between nursing and technology, empowering nurses to use digital tools effectively and safely.
